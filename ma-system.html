00 — Chylox Project
What is Chylox Project?
The research and development initiative behind MA System, MAKI OS, ARARA, and the papers. Everything here is part of it.

Chylox Project is a human-AI systems research project exploring how humans and AI agents can be connected through memory, permission, runtime control, and safety boundaries.

The central question is not "how do we make AI smarter?" but "how do we design the space between human judgment and AI execution to be structured, controllable, and auditable?"

Chylox Projectは、人間とAIエージェントの「あいだ」を設計する研究・開発プロジェクトです。
MA SystemはそのなかでAIの実行・記憶・権限・安全を担う中核システムとして位置づけられています。

01 — MA System
What is MA System?
The core execution framework of Chylox Project. Named after a concept that has no direct translation in English.
間
MA
space · pause
relationship · interval
the connection between
two things

The name has no exact translation.

間 (ma) is a Japanese concept describing the space, pause, or relationship between two things. It appears in 人間 (ningen — "human being"): the space within what it means to be human. Not the individual, but the in-between.

MA System is designed to occupy that space — the ma between human judgment and AI execution. A layer where decisions are held, permissions are checked, memory is kept, and failures are caught before they propagate.

"Not just making AI smarter. Designing the space between humans and AI to be structured, controllable, and safe." AIをただ賢くするのではなく、人とAIのあいだを設計する。
記憶・権限・実行制御・安全境界を「間（ま）」に置くことで、AIエージェントを検証可能・運用可能にする。

MA System was built by Daisuke Tsunemori (Tsune2034).

For nearly a decade, he worked in a technical FSE role in the semiconductor field, standing at the front line between equipment, process behavior, and customer-side operation. The work included on-site technical sessions, NPI support, process map analysis, POR-based troubleshooting, uniformity trend analysis, and sigma-based variation control. The role sat closer to local technical support than routine field maintenance — identifying where hardware adjustment could correct process trends, and separating equipment-side from process-side root causes in the field.

This background shaped the design of MA System. In semiconductor field work, problems must be separated across hardware, process, operation, and human decision boundaries. MA System applies the same boundary-oriented thinking to AI agents: separating memory, permission, runtime control, and integration-dependent components so that human-AI execution becomes controllable and auditable.

No prior CLI or code experience before February 2026. The design comes not from software engineering tradition but from the discipline of making complex systems safe, observable, and recoverable in the field.

直近約10年は、半導体分野でテクニカル寄りのFSEとして、現地技術支援・技術セッション・NPI・量産機/開発機のPOR理解・プロセスマップ解析・均一性/Sigma管理に関わってきました。
ハードウェア、プロセス、運用、人間判断の境界を切り分ける考え方が、MA Systemの「責務分離」「境界設計」「安全な実行制御」「人間承認ポイント」の発想につながっています。
「コード職人」ではなく、「現場課題をAIシステム設計に変換する」立ち位置からのアーキテクチャ。

04 — System Architecture
MA System Connectivity Architecture
MA System runs on two CLI engines in parallel: Claude Code CLI (primary — hosts ARARA + ARIS) and Gemini CLI (standby — automatic failover + Google Workspace native).
Gemini CLI activates automatically on primary failure, and shares the same MCP bus.
2 CLIが並列稼働 — Claude Codeをプライマリ、Gemini CLIを自動切替先として設計。MCPバスを共有し、Google Workspace・Brave Search・PostgreSQL等17サービスに接続。
